This sight is to keep track of product you may need in the future. A sight built to show the validations and different features. Unfortunatly not the best looking. Please have fun and mess around while visiting.

Operating site

  • You can add a new product to the list by the button located bellow
  • You can not delete and item from the list until the quanity is changed to zero. I did this to give the feel of ordering items and you dont want the item removed until you have received the quanity you originally planned on.
  • To remove/delete item from list go to "edit" change the quanity to zero then click on details to delete.
